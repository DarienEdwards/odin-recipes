Tomato Mozzorella Salad

This is a recipe for tomato cheese salad!

Ingrediants

  • 2-3 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 oz. mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • fresh basil leaves, chopped or torn
  • 2 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate, alternating between them.
  2. Sprinkle the chopped or torn basil leaves on top of the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and balsamic vinegar until well combined.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the tomato and mozzarella salad.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve immediately.
