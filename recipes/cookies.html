Cookie Recipe

Description

This is a cookie receipe for the best chocolately goodness you ever tasted.

Ingredients List

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Cookies

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F
  2. Mix flour, baking sode, and salt in a bowl
  3. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar in a seperate bowl.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla extract to the butter mixture and beat until smooth.
  5. Add eggs and vanilla extract to the butter misture and beat until smooth.
  6. Gradually mix in the flour mixture
  7. Stir in chocolate chips
  8. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a lined baking sheet
  9. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
  10. Cool and enjoy!
