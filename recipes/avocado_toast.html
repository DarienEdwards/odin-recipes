Avovado Toast

Ingrediants

  • 1 Ripe Avocado
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, sliced tomatoes, feta cheese, or a drizzle of olive oil

Cooking Steps

  1. Toast two slices of bread until crispy
  2. Cut avocado in half lengthwise and remove the pit. Use a spoon to scoop out the flesh into bowl.
  3. Mash the avovado with a fork until its mostly smooth but still slight chunky.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado onto the toast, making sure to cover the entire surface.
  5. sprinkle the salt and pepper to taste over the top of the avocado.
  6. Add any addtional toppings you'd like such as sliced tomatoe chees or red pepper flakes.
