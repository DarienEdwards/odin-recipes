Jesse's Crystal Blue Cocktail

Description

Crystal Blue cocktail that will get you feeling good like... well you know, that other blue stuff.

Ingredients List

  • 2 ounces orange vodka
  • 1 ounce triple sec
  • 1 ounce blue curacao
  • 2 ounces orange infused simple syrup
  • Lemon juice, to taste

Steps

  1. Combine all ingrediants in a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Pour off the ice.
  3. Garnish with orange peel.
